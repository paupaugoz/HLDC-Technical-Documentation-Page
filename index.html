Introduction

The Hidalgo Land Development Corporation Materials Management System is a web application that incorporates the client's manual business processes, and automates it, while streamlining the business processes. The web application addresses the issues that the company had with data duplication through the use of Excel spreadsheets, and standardized the company's data to have everything kept in a centralized database. The materials management system consists of four modules:

  • Inventory Management

  • Reports Generation

  • Orders

  • Requisition

Technical Stack Used

  • Front end templating engine: Handlebars.js

  • Web application framework: Express.js

  • JavaScript runtime environment: Node.js

  • Database: MySQL

Key Concepts

  • Project

    • A project is referred to as the overall cluster of work. A project in HLDC's terms is a whole neighborhood being constructed.

  • Unit

    • A unit is referred to as the individual units, or houses in a given project. A house within a neighborhood

  • Purchase Orders

    • Purchase orders are forms that list down the list of materials needed by the engineers to purchase for inventory fulfillment

  • Requisition Forms

    • Requisition forms are forms made by the encoder that list down the materials needed by a project which is delivered from the warehouse to the project site.

  • Templates

    • Templates are made by management as a way to gauge budget by creating a house unit with a pre-set list of materials required to build it.

  • Bill of Materials

    • The bill of materials is the actual list of materials purchased for a given unit, and project.

  • Reports Generation

    • Generation of reports summarize purchase order data, as well as project data to give a summarized report of the projects, and purchase orders.

User Roles

  • Management

    • Management is in charge of general administrative matters, such as employee access controls, reviewing of purchase orders, creation of new projects, and templates, creation of materials, and generation of reports

  • Engineer

    • Engineer is responsible for looking over purchase orders, reviewing project status, and generating reports.

  • Warehouse

    • Warehouse personnel are in charged of keeping track of inventory in the system, as well as viewing purchase orders and requisition forms

  • Encoder

    • Encoder is responsible for encoding purchase order data, new materials, and creating new requisition forms.
